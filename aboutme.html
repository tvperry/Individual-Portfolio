About Me

Hi! My name is Tess and I am a product designer. I designer with empathy for the user always in mind. Currently, I’m studying User Experience Design at the Unversity of Michigan School of Information. You can usually find me drinking a lavender latte at the many coffee shops on campus, at Yost watching a hockey game little too aggressively or meandering around the streets of Ann Arbor looking for a dog to pet. If you’re interested in chatting, please reach out and check out the work I have done so far!
